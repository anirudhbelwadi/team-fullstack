CSR - Corporate Social Responsibility


Why CSR?

Corporate social responsibility (CSR) projects undertaken by various national/multinational companies look forward to educating the marginalized and the needy in order to deliver the deserved livelihood to these people by providing them with various opportunities; there is a high demand of organizations who can undertake these projects on a perfect scale and execute the inceptions laid out mutually.

Team FullStack aims to solve the problems on the spectrum and developing a pipeline that can fill these gaps and also take into account the Social Development Goals (SDGs) - 1, 4, 8, 9, 10, 11, 17 which are No poverty, Quality Education, Decent work and economic growth, Industry Innovation and Infrastructure, Reduced Inequalities, Sustainable cities and communities and partnership for the goals listed out by the United Nations Department of Economic and Social Affairs (Sustainable Development).

We cooperate with national and multinational companies on some common grounds to educate marginalized people of all ages and provide them with employment opportunities at Level 1.

Our Vision

In the upcoming years we see that India will rise as the leader in the world with a maximum workforce, for this, we need some young pioneers and entrepreneurs who can provide all sorts of opportunities to their generation.
We would like to be that pioneer/entrepreneur who can contribute to mankind and return something back to society.
